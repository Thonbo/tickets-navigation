LEGO Masters Academy

Where future LEGO Masters are made

Inspired by the hit TV series, LEGO Masters Academy is a hands-on workshop experience at LEGO House. Pick your level, spend 60 minutes building under the guidance of LEGO Play Agents, and take your creation home. Whether you're building your first pond or mastering advanced S.N.O.T. techniques — there's a level for every builder.

⏱ 60 minutes per session
👤 Ages 5+ (under 13 with adult)
🎁 Take your creation home
🎫 Requires entry ticket
💰 From 149 DKK
